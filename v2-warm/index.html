How long does a typical install take?+
Most single-floor jobs (800 – 1,500 sq ft) finish in 3 to 5 working days, including demo, subfloor prep, install, and trim. Whole-home projects with stairs run 7 to 10 days. You'll get a written timeline with your estimate.
Do you handle insurance claim work?+
Yes — about a third of our work is insurance-related. We work directly with adjusters from State Farm, USAA, Allstate, Farmers, Liberty Mutual and more — handling scope letters, supplements, photo documentation, and matching materials.
Will you move my furniture and appliances?+
Standard furniture and appliances (fridge, washer/dryer, beds, dressers) are part of the job. Pianos, gun safes, large aquariums, and antique pieces over 200 lbs should be moved by a specialist before we start.
Can you install over concrete or radiant heat?+
Yes to both. LVP, laminate, and most engineered hardwoods install cleanly over concrete with the right underlayment and moisture barrier. For radiant heat we'll spec a product rated for it — usually engineered hardwood.
How dusty is the install?+
Demo is the messiest stage. We seal off adjacent rooms with zip walls, run HEPA air scrubbers during cutting, and vacuum daily. For site-finished hardwood we use dust-containment sanders.
What's your warranty?+
Manufacturer warranty on materials (typically 25 years to lifetime depending on product), plus a 2-year installation warranty on our labor. We come back free for the first year if anything settles, pops, or needs adjusting.
¿Hablan español?+
Sí — Jorge y la mayoría del equipo hablan español. Estimados, comunicación, y documentos disponibles en español si lo prefiere.
