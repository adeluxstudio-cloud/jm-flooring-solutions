08 — FAQ

Common
questions.

Don't see yours? Text us at (720) 555·0144 — we usually respond within an hour during business days.

How long does a typical install take?+
Most single-floor jobs (800–1,500 sq ft) finish in 3–5 working days, including demo, subfloor prep, install, and trim. Whole-home projects with stairs run 7–10 days. We provide a written timeline with your estimate.
Do you handle insurance claim work?+
Yes. We work directly with adjusters from companies like State Farm, USAA, Allstate, and Farmers — handling scope letters, supplements, photo documentation, and matching materials. You only pay above your approved scope if you choose an upgrade.
Will you move my furniture and appliances?+
We move standard furniture and appliances as part of the job, including fridges, washers, dryers, and beds. Pianos, gun safes, and aquariums need to be moved by a specialist before we start.
Can you install over concrete or radiant heat?+
Yes to both. LVP, laminate, and most engineered hardwoods install cleanly over concrete with the right underlayment. For radiant heat we recommend engineered products rated for heat — we'll spec the right one with you.
How dusty is the install?+
Demo is the messiest stage. We seal off adjacent rooms with zip walls, run HEPA air scrubbers during cutting, and vacuum daily. For site-finished hardwood we use dust-containment sanders.
What's your warranty?+
Manufacturer warranty on materials (typically 25 years to lifetime depending on product) plus a 2-year installation warranty on labor. We come back free for the first year if anything settles or pops.
