How it works

From the first
measurement to
the final baseboard.

Most single-floor jobs are done in three to five working days. You'll know exactly what's happening — and who's doing it — every single hour we're on site.

01

Walkthrough & Estimate

On-site measurement, subfloor and moisture check, and a line-itemized written estimate within 24 hours. No high-pressure call, no "today only" pricing.

Day 0 Free · No obligation
02

Material Selection

Samples delivered to your house so you can see them against your walls, your lighting, your furniture. We help you weigh durability, look, warranty, and lead time.

Day 1 – 3 15+ samples

Demo & Subfloor Prep

Install & Final Walk
